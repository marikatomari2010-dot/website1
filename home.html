I am a Multicultural Student

OUR MISSION

This website aims to raise awareness about the challenges faced by multicultural students, including experiences of bullying, discrimination, and exclusion based on culture, ethnicity, language, or identity. By sharing information, real experiences, and educational resources, the site seeks to highlight the impact of multicultural bullying and hateful behavior. It also promotes understanding, empathy, and practical strategies to reduce bullying, challenge prejudice, and create safer, more inclusive school communities where every student feels respected and valued.
