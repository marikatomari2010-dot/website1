I am a Multicultural Student

YOU. ARE. REMARKABLE

Growing up between worlds isnt always easy, but it gives you a unique set of skills that are becoming the most valuable assets in our modern, globalized society. Here are just some of the advantages and benefits of being multicultural:

  • High Emotional Intelligence and Empathy

    Multicultural students often possess a heightened ability to read a room. That helps you empathize with diverse groups of people, making you an exceptional leader, teammate, and communicator.

  • Cultural Intelligence

    While monocultural students may struggle with culture shock or international etiquette, multicultural students are able to adapt quickly to new environments. In a professional world that is more interconnected than ever, that background is a massive competitive advantage.

  • Cognitive Flexibility and Problem-solving

    As multicultural students constantly navigate different cultural norms, languages, and social cues, their brains are always practicing cognitive flexibility. Thus, they are naturally better at thinking outside the box, and see multiple paths to solve problems based on different cultural frameworks.
